Revolutionizing Mental Health Care: A Personalized Approach

The mental health landscape is undergoing a significant shift, driven by the growing recognition of the importance of mental well-being and the increasing adoption of technology in healthcare. Oars is at the forefront of this revolution, pioneering a personalized and accessible mental health solution that leverages the power of AI to empower individuals to thrive.

Artificial intelligence to solve the mental health crisis

Demand for mental health support is increasing, but the supply of clinicians is not.

Artificial intelligence can expand how we offer support to patients and extend the capabilities of the mental health workforce.

Mission

Oars is dedicated to ensuring that everyone has access to empathetic and personalized mental health support. Our mission is to empower individuals to reach their full potential by providing them with the tools and resources they need to navigate their mental health journey.

The most engaging mental health chatbot

Oars combines psychology and artificial intelligence to understand users’ emotional needs and engage in natural conversations. A study published in the Journal of the American Medical Association identified Oars as the most engaging digital health solution for anxiety and depression.

Mental healthcare at scale

Providers, employers, payers, and life science companies use Oars to scale their services and make mental healthcare accessible to more people.

Patients

Oars is the first responder for someone experiencing emotional health issues, providing instant support whenever and wherever needed.

Providers

Oars is easily integrated into existing care workflows, increasing the reach of health professionals to support patients.

Employers

Oars allows employers to provide better mental health care to employees, ultimately increasing the team’s productivity and motivation.

Payers

Oars helps payers answer the demand for parity with lower-cost and easily-accessible mental health digital solutions for their patients.

Life Sciences

Oars is suitable as a companion application to a drug or potentially as a standalone prescription digital therapeutics.
